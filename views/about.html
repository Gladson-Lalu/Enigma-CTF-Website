ABOUTENIGMA 

So what's a CTF?
CTF or Capture The Flag is a 2 hour team tournament where the teams solve many challenges and find the clues hidden in various parts of our campus.
This CTF uses Quizzes and Riddles as Challenges
  • Quiz/Riddles : Quiz questions are the equivalent of trivia questions. One question that has one answer and the answers can be anything from a single word to a whole sentence, not being case sensitive.
Who can participate?
  1. Open for 1st and 2nd year students of AEC
  2. A team should consist of minimum 2 participants and maximum 3 students.
Requirements
It is mandatory that you fill the registration form provided to participate in the CTF.
How to play?
  1. Fill the registration form provided to you in the beginning of the CTF, you will receive a token in your email after submitting it.
  2. Head on to the ENIGMA register portal and register your team there using the token received above.
  3. The event will consist of 3 levels. Each level containing multiple questions.
  4. After answering the kick-off question, you will receive enter into level 1. You will receive some clues which will lead you to the first set of locations. Once you scanned all the QR codes from the locations of the first level, you will receive the clues to the locations of the next level.
  5. The team that reaches the final destination scanning the most QR will be declared the winner.
