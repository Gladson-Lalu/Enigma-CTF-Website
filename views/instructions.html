INSTRUCTIONS 

Now that you are a part of Enigma, you must know of some rules we follow around here.

General Rules and Instructions
  • No cooperation between teams with independent accounts.
  • Absolutely no sabotaging of other competing teams, or in any way hindering their independent progress.
  • When you start the CTF, 2 hours will be allotted to complete the challenges.
  • Each question has some points to it depending upon the difficulty.
  • Judging of the round will be based on two parameters. The first is the score of the question and the second is the time taken for the submission.
  • Flags found are of the format enigma{answer}. Make sure you submit it in the format specified. Flag text is not case-sensitive.
  • Ranks can be viewed on the hackerboard page. The leaderboard is updated every time a submission is made.
  • Feel free to explore the concept behind the problem on the internet.
  • It is a competitive environment aimed at enjoying fun-filled teamwork and getting to know your campus. Do not share flags and solutions with others. It will be considered as cheating.
  • Ensure all the members of the team participate and have fun
